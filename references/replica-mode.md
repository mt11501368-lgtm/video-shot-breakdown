# Imitation and replication mode

Answer: **How do I produce a similar effect?**

Turn every shot into a production instruction containing:

1. Exact time range and target duration.
2. What appears in frame.
3. Actor action, eyeline, entrance, exit, and prop handling.
4. Shot size, camera height, lens feel, and composition.
5. Camera movement with start and end states.
6. Edit point, transition, speed change, and motion direction.
7. Dialogue or narration with speaker and delivery.
8. Music intensity, ambience, and specific effects.
9. AI prompt with continuity anchors when the output is AI-generated.

Required workbook sheets:

- `复刻执行说明`
- `分镜脚本`
- `拍摄清单`
- `台词文案`
- `剪辑时间轴`

Quality bar:

- Make instructions observable and executable; avoid “拍得高级” or “增加氛围”.
- Keep screen direction and spatial geography consistent.
- Specify safe substitutes for breakaway props, weapons, heights, and stunts.
- For AI generation, lock character reference images and 3–5 invariant visual anchors per character.
- Describe exact cut triggers such as a line ending, impact frame, gaze shift, or footfall.
- Build the checklist from actual screenplay needs: locations, cast, wardrobe, props, effects, sound, and continuity.
